
## A

**AI maturity score**
A 0–3 score estimating public evidence of AI engagement.

## B

**Bench match**
Assessment of whether inferred prospect needs align with current delivery bench.

**Brief**
Short for hiring signal brief unless otherwise specified.

## C

**Claim ref**
A traceable reference tying an outbound statement to supporting evidence.

**Compaction**
Summarization and reduction of active context while preserving required state.

## D

**Draft**
A generated but not yet sent outbound message.

## E

**Evidence record**
Normalized source-backed record used to support claims and scoring.

## G

**Gap brief**
Competitor gap brief comparing prospect to peers.

## H

**Handoff**
Transfer of control from autonomous system to human operator.

## I

**ICP segment**
One of the fixed Tenacious ideal-customer-profile categories.

## K

**KB**
Knowledge base containing durable markdown research artifacts.

## L

**Lead**
Operational prospect object used by the system.

## O

**Orchestrator**
Primary coordinating agent.

## P

**Policy decision**
A recorded allow/block/escalate judgment under system safety rules.

## R

**Rehydration**
Reconstructing working context from durable state.

## S

**Session state**
Compact operational summary for continuing work on a lead.

**Signal**
Public-data-derived indicator about fit or timing.

## T

**Trace**
Top-level observability object covering a lead-processing run.
