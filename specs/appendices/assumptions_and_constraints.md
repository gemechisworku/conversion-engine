
## 1. Assumptions

* all challenge-time prospects are synthetic
* public-source collection is allowed within stated constraints
* email is the primary outbound channel
* SMS is secondary and reserved for warm/scheduling contexts
* a KB-backed architecture is preferred over raw repeated retrieval
* Langfuse or equivalent trace tooling is available
* the orchestration runtime can enforce tool permissions

---

## 2. Constraints

* no real prospect outreach during the challenge without approved routing
* no unsupported factual claims
* no staffing commitments beyond allowed authority
* no custom pricing negotiation by the system
* all side effects must be observable
* context must remain bounded through memory/compaction

---

## 3. Open Technical Biases

* markdown KB is preferred for interpretability
* typed tools are preferred over unstructured agent freeform actions
* specialized subagents are preferred for noisy or context-heavy work
